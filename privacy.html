Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Last Updated: January 2025

Simple Privacy Promise

Vaultic does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. As a simple password generator app for Android, we believe your privacy should never be compromised.

What We DON'T Collect

  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, email addresses, device IDs, or any identifying information.
  • No Generated Passwords: Passwords you generate are never stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere.
  • No Usage Analytics: We don't track how you use the app, what passwords you generate, or when you use it.
  • No Device Data: We don't access or collect information about your Android device, OS version, or hardware.
  • No Network Activity: The app works completely offline and makes no network requests.

How Your Privacy is Protected

  • Local Storage Only: App preferences and settings are stored locally on your Android device only.
  • Offline Operation: Vaultic works entirely offline - no internet connection required or used.
  • No Third-Party Services: We don't integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or any external services.
  • No Ads: Completely ad-free experience means no advertising trackers or data collection.

App Permissions

Vaultic requests minimal Android permissions and only those necessary for core functionality. We do not request permissions for accessing contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any sensitive device features.

Your Data Control

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing for us to delete, modify, or share. You maintain complete control over your information at all times. If you uninstall Vaultic from your Android device, all local app data is automatically removed.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: info@quantixsoft.com

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our privacy practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Since Vaultic operates offline, we recommend checking this page periodically for updates.
