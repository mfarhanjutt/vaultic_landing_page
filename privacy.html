Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22 July 2025

Last Updated: 22 July 2025

Simple Privacy Promise

Vaultic does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data with third parties. As a password generator and secure storage app for Android, we believe your privacy should never be compromised.

What We DON'T Collect

  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, email addresses, device IDs, or any identifying information.
  • No Generated Passwords: Passwords you generate are never transmitted or logged anywhere outside your device.
  • No Stored Passwords: Your saved passwords remain encrypted on your device only - we cannot access them.
  • No Usage Analytics: We don't track how you use the app, what passwords you generate, or when you use it.
  • No Device Data: We don't access or collect information about your Android device, OS version, or hardware.
  • No Network Activity: The app does not send or receive data over the internet, except when opening external links (e.g., our privacy policy) in your device's browser.

How Your Privacy & Security is Protected

  • Local Storage Only: All preferences, settings, and saved passwords are stored locally on your Android device only.
  • Strong Encryption: Saved passwords are encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods, ensuring only you can access them.
  • Offline Operation: Vaultic works entirely offline - no internet connection required or used for core functionality.
  • No Third-Party Services: We don't integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or any external services.
  • No Ads: Completely ad-free experience means no advertising trackers or data collection.
  • Secure by Design: Your encryption keys are generated securely and never stored in plain text.

App Permissions

Vaultic requests minimal Android permissions and only those necessary for core functionality. We do not request permissions for accessing contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any sensitive device features. The app only requires basic storage permissions to save your encrypted password vault locally.

Data Storage & Encryption

When you choose to save passwords in Vaultic, they are encrypted using proven cryptographic methods and stored locally on your device. This means:

  • Local Only: Your encrypted password vault never leaves your device
  • Strong Security: Industry-standard encryption protects your data
  • No Cloud Sync: Your passwords are not backed up to any cloud service
  • You Control Access: Only you can decrypt and access your saved passwords
  • Named Organization: Each saved password can be named for quick reference and easy management

Your Data Control

Since we don't collect your data remotely, there's nothing for us to delete, modify, or share from our servers. You maintain complete control over your information at all times. If you uninstall Vaultic from your Android device, all local app data including your encrypted password vault is automatically removed.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: info@quantixsoft.com

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our privacy practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Since Vaultic operates offline, we recommend checking this page periodically for updates.
